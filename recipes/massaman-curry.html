Massaman Curry

Home

Description

A hearty thai curry that is milder on the spiciness level than most of its counterparts. The curry paste can be homemade, but that would require a lot more harder ingredients that are typically harder to get than simply buying the premade paste.

Ingredients

  • Thai massaman curry paste
  • Coconut milk
  • jasmine/fragrant white rice
  • Potatos
  • Meat or Tofu
  • Fried Peanuts

Steps

  1. Cube potatos into slightly larger than bite-sized pieces
  2. Cube or chop your proteins into bite sized pieces
  3. Cook your proteins separately to a slightly undercooked level and remove from pan
  4. Add curry paste, and water into deep pan
  5. Add coconut milk to desired sweetness, mildness, and consistency
  6. Toss in potatos
  7. Simmer sauce and potatos on low heat until potatos are soft and easy to pierce with a fork
  8. Add in your partially cooked proteins to the sauce
  9. Continue to simmer for a few more minutes until proteins are properly cooked
  10. Remove pan from heat
  11. Serve curry over white rice and garnish with fried peanuts

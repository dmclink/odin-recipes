Teriyaki Tofu

Home

Description

Declious vegan recipe with minimal required ingredients. Pairs great with rice and any blanched vegetables you might find in the fridge.

Ingredients

  • Tofu
  • Soy Sauce
  • Mirin
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Slice tofu into thin blocks
  2. Pan fry or grill tofu
  3. Prepare sauce in mixing bowl by combining soy sauce, mirin, and Sugar
  4. Simmer sauce until thick
  5. Drizzle thickened sauce over cooked tofu
  6. Garnish with any bonito flakes or seasonings to taste or serve directly over rice
  7. Enjoy!

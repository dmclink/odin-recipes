Description

Perfectly fried rice. Great way to use up the leftovers from the days before.

Ingredients

  • White Rice
  • Soy Sauce
  • Black Pepper
  • Tofu

Steps

  1. Cook white rice, preferably the day before and store in refrigerator. If cooking day of, use a little less water than usual
  2. If rice was refrigerated prior, leave out on the counter long enough to warm to room temperature
  3. Cut tofu into small cubes
  4. Fry tofu with desired seasonings
  5. Once tofu is cooked, place into a separate plate on the side
  6. Get a pan (or wok if you have it!) piping hot
  7. Spread a tablespoon of oil in the pan
  8. Throw in the fried rice and lower fire to medium
  9. Spread the rice into an even layer on the pan bottom
  10. Season the rice with black pepper
  11. Cook until you hear the rice start to crackle. Note this is a slightly different sound than the popping of the moisture from the rice hitting the oil. If the pan was hot enough this should only take a couple minutes
  12. Splash soy sauce directly onto the pan surface (on a spot there is no rice, could be the edge as it will be hot enough) to immediately steam off the liquid so it doesn't wet the rice while we still get the flavor
  13. Crank up the heat on the stove again and begin to toss the rice breaking up clumps that may have formed with a spatula
  14. Toss in the cooked tofu to get it back up to cooked temperature again
  15. This last process after turning up the heat shouldn't take more than another minute or two
  16. Remove all rice from heat, garnish with green onion, and serve
